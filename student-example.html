    Career Services

    Johns Hopkins Student Assistance Program (JHSAP)

    The Johns Hopkins Student Assistance Program (JHSAP) is a life management resource that can help you identify and manage challenging issues in healthy ways. Getting help is free, convenient, and confidential. Services include short-term counseling, crisis response, healthy relationship support, and school-life coaching and adjustment.

    Learn more

    Disability Services

    Johns Hopkins University is committed to providing reasonable accommodations for students with documented disabilities. Student Disability Services (SDS) partners with students to ensure they have the accommodations they need to be successful. To learn more or register with SDS, visit our website.

    Library Services

    The JHU library possesses over 3.8 million books, a million e-books, newspapers and hundreds of databases that students can access in person at Homewood or virtually. JHU Libraries love helping students find appropriate materials, learn how to research effectively, and ensure that they are using appropriate citation techniques. You can meet with them one-on-one, but the librarian also host workshops and webinars.

    Learn more
